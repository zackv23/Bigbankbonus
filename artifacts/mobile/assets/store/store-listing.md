## Full Description (4000 chars max)

**Stop leaving money on the table. BigBankBonus helps you find, track, and maximize bank account bonuses — all in one place.**

Banks offer hundreds of dollars in signup bonuses every year, but the rules are complex, deadlines are tight, and missing a single requirement means losing out. BigBankBonus is your personal bank bonus command center, powered by AI.

---

### Discover Profitable Bonuses

Browse a curated feed of the best bank account bonuses available right now. See the exact requirements, bonus amounts, deadlines, and difficulty ratings before you commit. Filter by bonus size, effort level, or bank to find the opportunities that fit your lifestyle.

- Bonuses ranging from $200 to $1,000+
- Clear requirement breakdowns (direct deposit, spending, balance)
- Deadline tracking so you never miss a payout
- Updated regularly with the latest offers

---

### Connect Your Accounts

Link your bank accounts securely to automatically track your bonus progress. See exactly how much direct deposit you've completed, how many qualifying transactions remain, and whether you're on track to earn each bonus — without logging into multiple bank websites.

- Secure, read-only bank connections
- Real-time progress bars for each bonus requirement
- Multi-bank support: Chase, Citi, Wells Fargo, Bank of America, and more
- Instant alerts when you hit a milestone

---

### AI Financial Coach

Your personal AI coach monitors your bonus progress and gives you actionable advice in plain English. Ask it anything — "Am I on track for my Chase bonus?" or "What should I do this week?" — and get a clear, personalized answer.

- Proactive reminders before deadlines
- Personalized strategy recommendations
- Natural language Q&A about your accounts
- Weekly bonus earnings summaries

---

### Autopay Scheduler & Calendar

Never miss a payment deadline or bonus milestone again. The built-in calendar shows all your upcoming requirements, autopay dates, and bonus unlock events in one clean view. Schedule reminders and set autopay rules to keep everything on track automatically.

- Monthly and weekly calendar views
- Color-coded events by bank and bonus type
- Autopay scheduling with confirmation alerts
- Integration with your device calendar

---

## EAS Submit — Track Promotion Notes

The first upload to Google Play should use the `"internal"` track (already configured in `eas.json`).
Promotion path after internal testing:

1. **Internal** (`track: "internal"`) — Upload and test with up to 100 internal testers
2. **Alpha** (`track: "alpha"`) — Expand to closed testing group
3. **Beta** (`track: "beta"`) — Open testing or larger closed group
4. **Production** (`track: "production"`) — Full public release

To change the track: update `"track"` in `eas.json` under `submit.production.android`, then run:
```
eas submit --platform android --profile production
```
Or promote directly in the Google Play Console without re-submitting a build.

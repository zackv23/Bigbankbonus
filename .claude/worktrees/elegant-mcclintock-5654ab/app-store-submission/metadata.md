## Full Description
*(≤4000 characters)*

BigBankBonus is the smartest way to find, track, and earn bank account bonuses — the free cash banks pay you just for opening an account and completing simple requirements.

**Find the Best Bonuses**
Browse a curated, always-updated database of bank bonuses from national banks, regional institutions, and online-only banks. Filter by bonus amount, minimum deposit, time to complete, and geographic availability. Never miss a lucrative offer again.

**AI Agent — Your Personal Bonus Coach**
Our built-in AI Agent analyzes your current bonus stack, answers your questions about requirements, and proactively alerts you when a step is due. Just ask: "Which bonuses expire this month?" or "What do I need to do to earn the Chase bonus?" and get an instant, accurate answer.

**Calendar & Scheduler**
Every bonus has deadlines — minimum balance hold periods, direct deposit requirements, debit card swipe minimums. BigBankBonus automatically builds a personalized schedule of exactly what to do, and when, so you can complete every requirement on time and collect every dollar.

**Accounts Dashboard**
Track all your active bonus applications in one place. See how much you've earned, how much is pending, and which requirements are still outstanding. Your real-time bonus pipeline at a glance.

**Analytics**
Understand your bonus earning history with clean charts showing total earned, bonuses completed, and projected future earnings. Set goals and watch your progress.

**Secure by Design**
Your financial data never leaves your device unencrypted. BigBankBonus uses Face ID / Touch ID authentication and secure on-device storage. We never sell your data.

**Credits System**
Power users can unlock advanced AI Agent features with in-app credits — no hidden fees, pay only for what you use.

Whether you're a seasoned "bank bonus churner" or just discovered that banks pay you to open accounts, BigBankBonus gives you the information, automation, and coaching to maximize every dollar.

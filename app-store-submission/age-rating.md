## Gambling Note — Important

**BigBankBonus does not involve gambling.**

The app helps users discover and track **bank account opening bonuses** — legitimate cash incentives offered by FDIC-insured banks to customers who open checking or savings accounts. These are standard financial promotions, not gambling:

- No element of chance or random outcome determines whether a bonus is earned
- Bonuses are paid by banks upon completion of specific, deterministic requirements (e.g., "make 10 debit card purchases within 90 days")
- The app does not facilitate wagering, betting, or games of chance
- No real or virtual currency is used for gambling purposes
- The Stripe credit system is a straightforward in-app purchase for AI Agent usage, not a gambling mechanism

Apple's gambling category covers "real money gambling, poker, lottery, or other games where actual money is exchanged." BigBankBonus does not meet this definition.

## Rationale

BigBankBonus is a personal finance information and productivity app. Its content is equivalent to a spreadsheet or a financial news app — it displays information about publicly available bank promotions and helps users track their own activity. There is no content that would be inappropriate for any age group.

The AI Agent feature generates text responses about financial topics (bank bonuses, account requirements, banking strategy). OpenAI's API applies content filtering; the responses are constrained to the financial domain and do not generate inappropriate content.
